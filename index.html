Welcome to My Academic Portfolio

Hello! I am Qianhong Yang, a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the National University of Singapore (NUS). My research focuses on integrating (computational) fluid mechanics, active matter physics, and machine learning to study the collective motion of active particles at low Reynolds numbers.
